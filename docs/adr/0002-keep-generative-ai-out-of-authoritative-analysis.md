# Keep Generative AI Out of Authoritative Analysis

AccessiRoom derives Analysis Findings, Arrangement Status, and Layout Scores deterministically from captured spatial evidence and the Operator-confirmed Mobility Profile so every result remains reproducible and explainable. A future generative assistant may communicate established results, deterministic counterfactuals, and ways to resolve uncertainty in plain language, but it must not create, override, independently score, assign probabilities to, or predict likely analysis outcomes; this trades open-ended advice for trustworthy traceability.
